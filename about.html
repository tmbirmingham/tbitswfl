About TBIT SWFL

TBIT SWFL was founded by Tim Birmingham to provide reliable, security-focused IT support to homes and small businesses throughout Southwest Florida.

With experience in networking, system administration, and cybersecurity, TBIT SWFL delivers practical technology solutions without the corporate complexity.

Our mission is simple: make technology secure, reliable, and stress-free.
